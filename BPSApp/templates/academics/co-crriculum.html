Rules



The Co-curricular activities bring in a sense of competitive spirit to compete with self-first and then with peers adding to the holistic nature of the curriculum for the benefit of the students. Extra-Curricular Activities also contribute to the over all development of the individual. Participation in activities of their passion can increase the brain function, help concentrate and manage their time better, all of which contribute to higher grades. High endurance sports, for example, will train students to focus and build stamina in the face of intense difficulty. This gives them an advantage when it comes to studying and taking exams. For the benefit of the students, provisions for the following extra- curricular activities have been made in the school premises.


Every Saturday CCA Periods and competitions are conducted to boost the team spirit and enhance the inherent talent among the students.


Yoga and meditation is practiced daily during morning assembly, to help the students to improve concentration and control over their feelings


Physical training classes are conducted for the right physical development of every child.


News hour during daily assembly keeps the children abreast of current affairs


Both modern and ancient games are played with required sports kits and guidance


Skits and drama competitions are conducted regularly to spread cultural awareness among the students


Classical and other various types of performing arts are organized


Educational tours, exhibitions and picnics are regularly arranged to make the children understand the unity in diversity of India


Symposiums, seminars and workshops are arranged and conducted regularly to have the students well informed of the latest happenings in the world


Debates, elocution and quiz competitions are organized frequently to enable the children to improve their knowledge, thinking and assessing skills


Foundation and Bridge Courses are provided for different competitive examinations and Olympiads


Exposure to the various activities of the United Nations to enable them to understand their utility, the role of every individual innation building and participation.



Evaluation System


We at Bhavana Public School believe that motive of the evaluation system is analysing the student thoroughly for the feedback and it was discussed individually with the parents in PTM.


4 Evaluations for Grade I to V.


First Term and one annual Exam per academic year for Grade VI to VIII with 2 periodic tests- each in one term


Pre Mid Term, Mid Term, Post Mid Term and Final Exam for Grade IX and X with weekly and monthly exam. For X & XII- Pre annual exams with the chapter wise last ten years board question papers is solving during this special classes.


only 35-40 students per class enable teacher to pay individual attention to wards the students.


With the help of Breadth and Balance, we are getting the exact subject knowledge which should be accepted by the students in particular class.


In the Question Paper Analysis- each and every students Answer sheet should be analyzed. It helps us to know the performance status of the child for each question and its concept. If the maximum students concept is not clear, it is re-teach in the class. Usage of audio- visual software and teaching aids clear the students’ concepts.


Student centered daily assembly includes prayer, pledge, thought, on the character building sentences, News, quiz, activities and value based message by Principal and Teacher.


Always classroom supervision by school management committee through Principal ,Vice Principal & Academic Coordinators, Subject HODs .
