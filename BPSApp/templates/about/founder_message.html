Message

       The purpose of education is to ignite a spirit of inquiry, creativity, a thirst for knowledge, striving for excellence, character-building, self-development as well as cultivate good habits to last a life-time. Bhavana Public School strives tirelessly to mentor its students to realize their potential and develop confidence to face the future challenges in a fiercely competitive world moving at an accelerated pace. Every child is like a seed which has the potential to grow if it is nourished with love and care under the right guidance.

       BHAVANA PUBLIC SCHOOL is a child friendly school where each and every child is understood and valued. Personal attention is given to the development of children in all spheres; academics, sports and cultural. It is our constant endeavor to groom our children for life; making those well - rounded individuals who will be able to contribute to the society they live in, who shall set out to leave the world a better place than what they find it to be. We are always open for new ideas and suggestions which can add more value.
