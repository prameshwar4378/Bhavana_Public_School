Our Vision & Mission

  BHAVANA PUBLIC SCHOOL & JUNIOR COLLEGE, RISOD was established by the erudite daughter of the , Bhavana P.Gawali, under the aegis of the society of Mahila Utkarsh Pratisthans, Risod, August 10th - 2003, with a commitment to pay his dues to Risod and a vision to provide holistic education with international standards with Indian values for the children of Risod and its surrounding areas

        OUR VISION



BHAVANA PUBLIC SCHOOL aims to be an institution of merit, committed to producing leaders of the future, and creating an education system where all children get equal access to quality education.


        OUR MISSION



To promote individualistic learning by providing ample opportunities and a safe, congenial environment to students, so that they acquire the requisite skills and knowledge to become successful responsible global citizens.
