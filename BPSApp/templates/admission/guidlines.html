A student who wishes to take admission in a desired class needs to apply through the prescribed application form which is available in the school office. After scrutinizing the application, the school may inform the students about the admission accordingly depending upon the availability of the seats.


The students are required to submit the following documents along with application form: School leaving Certificate [last school attended]


Birth Certificate [if the child has not attended any school before]


An Affidavit [if the child is coming from an unrecognized school]


4 passport size photographs


1 passport size photo of each of the parents


Caste Certificate [if the child belongs to a reserved category].


Child Aadhar card xerox copy.
